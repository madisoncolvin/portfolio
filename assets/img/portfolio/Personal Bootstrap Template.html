Traditional Mediums

Madison Colvin

From a young age, art has always been an important part of my life. Creating and designing new things is a passion of mine and in my free time I love to draw with a variety of mediums to create cohesive, vibrant, and surreal designs. Some of my favorite mediums are:

  • Colored Pencils
  • Acrylic Paint
  • Oil Paint
  • Pen and Ink
  • Watercolor
  • Copic Markers

My parents used to tell me to figure out what you love to do and then make a career out of it. From a very young age, even before I started school, I was absorbed in dreaming up new ideas, faces, and places to show the world. Soon, it became clear that what many thought was just a “stage” or an “obsession,” was actually a passion of mine that would continue throughout my lifetime.
